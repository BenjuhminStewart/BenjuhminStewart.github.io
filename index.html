Our Mission

We've noticed that there are too many incidents and not enough police. That's why I took it upon myself to allow the community to report incidents amongst themselves and deal with the incidents that are a little less serious. We are currently tracking data with a test community to se if this app will yield lower crime rates as the app is used.
